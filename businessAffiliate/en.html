Invite Clients via Referral Link and Earn on Every Exchange

How to Start Earning?

  1. Register on the Bazonix platform and log in to your personal account.
  2. In the partner program section, set your desired commission percentage (within the available limits).
  3. Copy your unique referral link and share it with potential clients.

How Does the Referral System Work?

  • A user who follows your referral link is assigned to you for 1 year.
  • All exchanges made by this user during the year will bring you commission rewards.
  • Each time the user follows your referral link again, the 1-year period is renewed.
  • If the user follows another partner's referral link, all subsequent exchanges will be assigned to the new partner.

Partner Payouts

  • Partner payouts are made monthly through your personal account in USDT TRC20.
  • To receive a payout:
    1. Log in to your personal account.
    2. Go to the "Withdraw" tab.
    3. Enter your USDT TRC20 address to receive the funds.
    4. Click the "Withdraw" button.
  • After submitting a withdrawal request, the funds will be credited to the specified address within 3 days.

Benefits for Partners

  • Transparent reward tracking and accrual system.
  • Flexible commission percentage settings.
  • Automatic renewal of user assignment when they revisit your link.
  • Fast and convenient payouts in USDT TRC20.
